Value: 100 xp
Prerequisite: Willpower 35

Focus Power: Routine (+20) Willpower test
Range: 10 metres x psy rating
Sustained: 1 Action Point
Subtype: Attack

Effect: The psyker chooses a patch of fire in range and line of sight with a radius no greater than PR metres. Each round, he can either extinguish or control the fire.
